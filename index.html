I've been a photographer since 2019, but I've always loved telling stories. I started shooting freelance and never really could fully give it up.

Most of what I care about is people, who they are, how they move, and what their energy feels like in the exact moment I'm taking their photo. I shoot in a creative way, but still as an observer. I'm not trying to control everything or make people perform (unless they want to).

I pay attention to the in-between moments, the quiet parts, the little things that usually go unnoticed.

Modeling for other photographers and artists really expanded how I perceive photography. It helped me understand what it means to be both in front of and behind the camera, in a way that makes me more intentional when photographing others.

At the end of the day, I just want to tell a story.
